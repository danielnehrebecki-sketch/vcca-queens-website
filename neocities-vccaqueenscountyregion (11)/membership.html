Join Our Club

Membership in the VCCA Queens County Region is open to anyone with an interest in Chevrolet vehicles, whether or not you currently own one. We welcome enthusiasts of all ages who share our love for classic cars and community events.

Membership Benefits

  • Participation in local and national VCCA events
  • Access to technical expertise and restoration tips
  • Monthly newsletters with club news and features
  • Networking with fellow Chevrolet enthusiasts
