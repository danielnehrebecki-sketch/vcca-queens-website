About Our Club

Founded in 2001, the VCCA Queens County Region has been dedicated to preserving and celebrating Chevrolet automobiles from every era. Our members come from all walks of life, united by a shared passion for the beauty, engineering, and history of America’s most iconic vehicles.

Throughout the year, we host and participate in a variety of events, including car shows, cruises, community gatherings, and technical workshops. Whether you’re restoring your first Chevy or simply enjoy the nostalgia of the open road, you’ll find friendship, knowledge, and inspiration in our community.

Our club is part of the national Vintage Chevrolet Club of America, which connects enthusiasts worldwide and provides valuable resources for restoration and preservation efforts.
